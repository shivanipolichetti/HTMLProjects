Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes,1 cup plain yogurt,1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste,1 teaspoon chili powder,1 teaspoon turmeric powder,Salt to taste,2 tablespoons butter,1 cup tomato,1 cup heavy cream,1 teaspoon garam masala,Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

How to make:

  • Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour
  • In a pan, heat butter and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is browned.
  • Add tomato puree, heavy cream, and garam masala. Simmer until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with naan or rice.
